In the class called Open Style Lab, we team up to create a gourment for Rachel Handler.

Rachel Handler is an actress with a left leg prothetic leg. We started by interviewing Rachel and observing her daily activity and found out the pain point we want to work on.

We came up with the solution of transformative pants that allow Rachel easily configure the pants to decide whether or not to show her prothetic leg.

To better understand how to improve our design, we took it to Union Square and tested it out.

After that, we came up with a better way to wear the pants. The detailed information is shown in the video.
