This is a series of experiments on using inexpensive tools (Processing and projector) to create entertaining experience.

All backgrounds are written using Processing and then projected onto different surfaces.

Ⅰ

I found a beautiful pattern from one of package boxes. I used the computer camera to get the pattern and add the different color filter.

Together, it creates the mysterious feeling and I projected this on to my closet to take a portrait shot for my friend.

Ⅱ

I coded a night sky with stars moving on it. I asked my friends to mimic the pigeon using hands.

The whole secen seems calm and harmony.

Ⅲ

On my birthday, I coded the fireworks that can be generated by clicking the mouse.

The "happy birthday" is picture I found online and code it as background.

Ⅳ

Visuals are always projected on the white flat surface. What about other surfaces?.

So I coded a green background and projected onto the rug at home. Ane the rug is turned into a grass field!

See, isn't it easy to have fun?
